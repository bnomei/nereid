This spec provides a reusable character-grid canvas and Unicode drawing primitives.

Normative protocol reference: `docs/protocol-01.md`

## Requirements (EARS)

- THE SYSTEM SHALL render to a fixed-width character grid.
- THE SYSTEM SHALL support Unicode box-drawing characters by default.
- THE SYSTEM SHALL provide primitives to draw boxes, lines, and text with predictable collision behavior.
